Battle of Surabaya Award

Film animasi berjudul "Battle of Surabaya" kembali meraih penghargaan pada Amsterdam International Film Maker Festival. Film yang diproduksi tahun 2015 itu, memenangkan kategori Best Animation or Animated Sequence.

Musa, the story of a brave thirteen-year-old boy on November 10th 1945 war in Surabaya. A child who makes a living as shoe shiner, he is struggling to overcome for hard life in the era colonialism. He met the combatant, and inadvertent choosing a job as a courier. Such a job that bring him on adventure.
